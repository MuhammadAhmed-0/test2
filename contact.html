Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators really free to use?
Yes, all our calculator tools are completely free to use. No registration, no hidden fees, no premium features.
How accurate are your calculations?
Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are regularly tested for accuracy. However, results should be used for informational purposes and we recommend consulting professionals for important decisions.
Can I use these calculators on my mobile device?
Absolutely! All our calculators are fully responsive and work seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
Do you store my calculation data?
We prioritize your privacy. Calculation inputs and results are not stored on our servers. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
How can I request a new calculator?
We welcome suggestions! Use the contact form above to send us your ideas for new calculators you'd like to see on our platform.
Do you provide customer support?
Yes, we provide free support for all our tools. Contact us via email and we'll respond as quickly as possible, typically within 24 hours.
