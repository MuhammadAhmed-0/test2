How to Use the Split Bill Calculator

Our advanced split bill calculator makes it easy to divide expenses fairly among friends, family, or colleagues. Here's how to get the most out of it:

Quick Split vs Custom Split

  • Quick Split: Instantly divide the bill equally among 2, 3, or 4 people
  • Custom Split: Add unlimited people and customize how much each person owes
  • Item-by-Item: Add specific items and assign them to different people for precise splitting

Advanced Features

  • Tip & Tax Calculation: Automatically includes tip and tax in the split
  • Unequal Splitting: Some people can pay more or less based on what they ordered
  • Item Assignment: Assign specific menu items to specific people
  • Export Options: Share results via text, print, or copy to clipboard
  • Multiple Payment Methods: Track different payment methods for each person

Pro Tips

  • Use the item-by-item feature for complex restaurant bills
  • Set different tip percentages for different service levels
  • Export results to keep track of who owes what
  • Use custom percentages when people want to split unevenly
