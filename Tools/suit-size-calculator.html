How to Measure Correctly in Under Two Minutes

Waist is measured at the natural bend point above the hips. Inseam runs from the crotch seam to the bottom of the pant leg. If you already own well-fitting trousers, measure those instead. Waist and inseam matter most when pairing suit jackets with matching pants.
