What is an Anniversary Calculator?

This tool quickly calculates the time since your special date and shows you how many years, months, days, and even minutes you’ve shared! It also tells you your next big milestone (like 5 years, 1000 days, etc) and helps you celebrate more meaningfully.

  • Works for wedding anniversaries, dating, work anniversaries, or any occasion!
  • Shows time passed in all units, upcoming milestones, and fun facts.
  • Personalize by adding names to make it extra special.
