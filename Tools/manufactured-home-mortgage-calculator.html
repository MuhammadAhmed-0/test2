Understanding Manufactured Home Mortgages

Manufactured homes offer an affordable path to homeownership, but financing can be different from traditional mortgages. Here's what you need to know:

  • Loan Types: Chattel loans (personal property) vs. real estate loans (land + home)
  • Down Payment: Typically 5-20% depending on the loan program
  • Interest Rates: May be higher than traditional mortgages
  • Debt-to-Income Ratio: Lenders prefer DTI under 36%, but some allow up to 50%
  • Credit Requirements: Minimum credit score usually 620-640
  • PMI: Private Mortgage Insurance may be required with less than 20% down

Use this calculator to estimate your monthly payments and determine if you qualify for financing. Always consult with a qualified mortgage professional for personalized advice.
